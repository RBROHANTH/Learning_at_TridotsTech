Predator protection

Practical measures for your homestead

🦅

Hawk protection

  • String fishing line or wire in a grid overhead — hawks won't dive through tight spaces
  • Plant dense cover bushes so birds can shelter instantly
  • Your rooster is your early-warning system — alarm-calls at aerial threats
  • Restrict free-range to mid-morning; avoid early dawn & dusk (peak hawk hours)
🦦

Mongoose protection

  • Hardware cloth buried 30 cm underground around coop perimeter
  • Lock the coop door every single night — mongooses hunt at dusk and before dawn
  • Automatic timer coop door is worthwhile (₹1,500–3,000)
  • Remove leftover feed from the yard each evening
🐕

Dog & cat control

  • Solid 4-foot fence with buried wire base keeps dogs out
  • Young chicks are cat targets — keep in covered brooder until 4+ weeks
  • Motion-sensor lights near the coop deter night-time approaches
